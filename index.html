Roadmap
Phase 0 (Minting Incentives):
  • 0%: Public sale is enabled, go mint some Peppermints!
  • 25%: Community giveaways are unlocked! Join us in our Discord server for chances to win some prizes such as SOL, high-rarity Peppermints, and even some custom 1/1s!
  • 50%: The team's custom tools are made open source! These tools cover all aspects of creating an NFT collection, from generating traits with rarity tiers, producing the images, and tracking rarity!
  • 75%: $20,000 is allocated to the community wallet. For a breakdown of how we plan to manage community funds from there, please check out the "About" section of our site!
  • 100%: An additional $20,000 is allocated into the community wallet. WAGMI!
Phase 1 (Q1 2022):
  • Staking is released! By staking your Peppermints and removing them from the trading pool, you will be rewarded daily with our governance token, $MNTCOIN (name subject to change). These tokens will act as additional voting power for community decisions, as well as act as currency for future projects produced by the team or community.
  • Web interfaces are released for the previously-released tools, allowing for them to be utilized by anybody without any setup time or technical skill. The first community vote will be held to determine if any monetization will be added (i.e. banner ads, ad required before receiving finished product, etc). 100% of profits, if applicable, will go directly to the community wallet.
  • The team begins development on a custom marketplace for Peppermints. This will allow Peppermints to be sold and traded directly from our website, with no additional market fees! (Note: this feature is already well into production, but profits from the mint will be allocated to purchase third-party audits to ensure that our smart contract is completely secure)
  • Community Project #1 begins! Another community vote will be held to decide what this project should be, and interested community members will be divided into teams based on their skillsets. Community funds will be used to compensate members who contribute to the project, as well as all profits returning directly to the community wallet. More information about how we plan to run these community projects can be found in the "About" section.
Phase 2 (Q2 2022):
  • An artist will be commissioned to convert the Peppermints to 3D (out of the team's pocket, no community funds)! These models will be fully rigged and animated, allowing for them to be used as avatars in whatever Metaverse projects we decide to get involved in.
  • The team begins developing a JS library for easily creating a web3-enabled website for your NFT collection. This library will contain everything present on our website, as well as any additional utility the community desires.
  • A community vote is held to gauge the community's views on releasing a derivative project to allow more members to join. If we are in support of it, additional votes will be conducted to determine the total amount, process of creating the derivates (breeding, mutating, etc), and price.
  • Progress on Community Project #1 is evaluated. Compensation will be paid out based on contributions, and any particularly hard-working members will be offered paid positions on the team, if desired. Based on progress, we will either hold a vote on restructuring the community development or determining what Community Project #2 will be.
Phase 3 (Q3 2022):
  • A set of 1/1, honorary Peppermints are created by our artists. These will be given away to various high-influence crypto personalities and celebrities to bring more attention to our community. We expect that our constant work and utility added to the project up to this point will keep the floor steadily rising, and this should only serve to rocket it even further!
  • Community brainstorming begins for a Play-2-Earn game for Peppermint holders. The team knows that this is no small process, and also one that is consistently listed on roadmaps with no thought, which is why we have this listed so far down the road. Game creation is not our specialty so we plan to partner with an existing game studio to produce a quality product. More info on this as we get closer!
  • A community vote is held regarding our first in-person meetup! This will be voted on in an attempt to hold the meetup in a central location, allowing as many community members as possible to attend! Further details will also emerge as this date closer, but we can promise you now that there will be plenty of special goodies (digital and physical) for attendees!
  • Progress on Community Project #2 is evaluated. Compensation will be paid out based on contributions, and any particularly hard-working members will be offered paid positions on the team, if desired. Based on progress, we will either hold a vote on restructuring the community development or determining what Community Project #3 will be.
The work doesn't end here, more roadmap phases to be revealed as the community grows!
